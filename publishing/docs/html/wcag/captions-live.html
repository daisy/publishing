About

Number: 1.2.4
Level: AA
Applicability: Live streaming video
Requirement:

Captions are provided for all live audio content in synchronized media.
Guidance:
  • Understanding Success Criterion 1.2.4%
  • How to meet Success Criterion 1.2.4

Purpose

The purpose of this success criterion is to ensure that users have access the auditory content when accessing a real-time video.

How to Meet

This success criterion is typically not applicable to publications. If authors or publishers host a live streaming video event (e.g., a meet and greet session), they do so outside the context of their publications.

EPUB, for example, does not support live streaming protocols.

As a general web accessibility principle, however, an author or publisher hosting such a live streaming event needs to ensure that the auditory portion is accessible (e.g., through the use of real-time captioning).
