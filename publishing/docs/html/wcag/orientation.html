About

Number: 1.3.4
Level: AA
Applicability:
  • Fixed Layouts
Requirement:

Content does not restrict its view and operation to a single display orientation, such as portrait or landscape, unless a specific display orientation is essential.
Guidance:
  • Understanding Success Criterion 1.3.4
  • How to meet Success Criterion 1.3.4

Purpose

The purpose of this success criterion is to ensure that users can read the content regardless of the orientation of their device. If the user cannot hold the device but requires it to be mounted, for example, changing the orientation is not always possible.

How to Meet

To meet this success criterion, ensure that the content remains readable regardless of whether the device is in portrait or landscape orientation.

This success criterion typically is not an issue for reflowable content as, by nature, this type of content adapts to orientation changes.

Fixed layouts, on the other hand, are generally difficult to impossible to make conformant with this requirement because their layouts depend on a specific orientation.

Additional Information

The knowledge base does not currently include information on creating fixed layouts that can adapt to orientation changes due to a lack of viable techniques at this time.
